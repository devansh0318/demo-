Established 2018

Our Story

What started as a modest experiment in a small garage has blossomed into a community hub for coffee enthusiasts. Our journey began with a single vintage roaster and a simple mission: to treat every bean with the respect it deserves.

We focus on small batches to ensure maximum freshness and quality control. Every roast is a dialogue between the roaster and the bean, unlocking hidden notes of stone fruit, dark chocolate, and blooming jasmine.

Our beans are ethically traded, fostering direct relationships with farmers who share our passion for the perfect cup. We don't just sell coffee; we curate an editorial experience of flavor.
